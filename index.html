About Herty's Needle

Herty's Needle blends traditional artisanal tailoring with contemporary design. Our atelier crafts made-to-measure garments and offers precise alteration services — ensuring every client experiences exceptional fit and timeless style.

We believe in sustainable fashion: repairing and refining garments to extend their life while crafting new pieces with careful material selection.
